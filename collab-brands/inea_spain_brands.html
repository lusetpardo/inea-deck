INEA's Spain Brands for Collab · July 2026

Spain-based brands & venues for exclusive charm collaborations

A working shortlist for INEA, split into two outreach tracks: Spain-based fashion brands, and Spain's leading spa & wellness hotels and centers. The fashion track is sized up against your own reference names (LUPO, GIMAGUAS, HEREU, THINKING MU, BOBO CHOSES, SARDA); everything is checked against current status as of July 2026.

Positioning
Premium & established luxury · emerging designer · affordable/accessible luxury · silent luxury
Fashion categories
Clothing · shoes & bags · accessories & jewelry · swimwear & resortwear
Geography

Clothing

11 brands

Ready-to-wear where a charm reads as a hang-tag, zipper-pull, or knitwear tag collab — strongest with brands that already lean into small tactile details.

Thinking Mu

Affordable luxury
Barcelona · knitwear & ready-to-wear ★ your list
Mediterranean, artisanal-leaning knitwear and separates with a strong textile identity.
Knit tag charm tied to a signature cardigan or sweater drop.

Paloma Wool

Emerging designer
Barcelona · art-fashion ready-to-wear (founded 2014)
Paloma Lanna's small-batch label blurring art, photography and fashion — hand-drawn prints, sculptural knitwear, cult international following.
Art-print charm pulled from a seasonal photo/collage story.

Nice Things Paloma S.

Affordable luxury
Barcelona · contemporary womenswear (founded 1995)
Family-run label started by Paloma Santaolalla and Miguel Lanna — three decades of print-led, detail-oriented ready-to-wear across Spain, France and Portugal. (Paloma Wool's founder is their daughter.)
Signature-print charm as a loyalty gift for repeat shoppers.

Bobo Choses

Affordable luxury
Barcelona · playful ready-to-wear ★ your list
Illustration-driven, collectible prints; adult and kids lines with a cult following.
Illustrated charm as a wearable extension of a print capsule.

Palomo Spain

Córdoba / Madrid · genderfluid couture

Statement runway-adjacent charm for a capsule or red-carpet moment.

LAAGAM

Charm bundled with a signature trouser or blazer launch.

Founded by Blanca Miró and María de la Orden; bold prints, offbeat cuts, strong editorial following.
Limited charm co-drop tied to a seasonal print story.

Charm made from recycled metal, matching the brand's material story.

Keyring or bag charm as a menswear accessory add-on.

Charm as part of a made-to-measure or capsule gifting line.

Shoes & Bags

14 brands

The most natural charm category — zipper pulls, keyring charms, and bag charms are already a native accessory here. Mietis, Moi & Sass, Reliquiae and Manuel Dreesmann are the closest direct comparables to LUPO: small-studio, sculptural, made-in-Spain bag labels at a similar scale.

Hereu

Premium / established
Mallorca / Barcelona · leather bags & shoes ★ your list
Founded 2014; family-run Spanish ateliers, Mediterranean forms, now genuinely global "silent luxury" leather house.
Signature bag charm variant matching a hero bag silhouette.
hereustudio.com

LUPO

Premium / established
Barcelona · bags (est. 1950 boutique) ★ your list
Family house since the 1920s; Gaudí-inspired sculptural pleating (the MIPEL-award-winning Abanico), handcrafted in Ubrique.
Charm echoing the Abanico's pleated silhouette as a keychain miniature.

Mietis

Emerging designer
Barcelona · sculptural bags (founded 2017)
María Fontanellas' studio label — bold color, architectural silhouettes, made in Barcelona and Ubrique, €300–500 range.
Closest LUPO peer — a colour-matched charm for a seasonal Mietis drop.

Moi & Sass

Emerging designer
Madrid · bags (founded 2018)
Founded by Moira Laporta and Alessandra de Osma; sculptural yet wearable shapes, made in Spain from LWG-certified leather.
Charm as a co-branded gift with a signature investment-bag purchase.

Reliquiae

Premium / established
Madrid / Gijón · architectural bags (since 2011)
Héctor Jareño's studio, built on his father's 1957 leatherworking atelier; clean, proportion-driven, couture-referenced design — explicitly open to collaborations.
Directly collaboration-minded — strong first-outreach candidate.

Manuel Dreesmann

Affordable luxury
Barcelona · bags (Atelier Madre, founded 2018)
Direct-to-consumer minimalist leather bags handmade in Barcelona at an accessible price point.
Entry-price charm bundle for a younger gifting audience.

Miista

Emerging designer
Made in Spain/Portugal · footwear
Avant-garde, sculptural shoes handmade by artisans; worn by Charli XCX and Kendall Jenner.
Sculptural charm echoing a heel or sole detail.

Carmina Shoemaker

Premium / established
Mallorca (Inca) · heritage footwear
Long-running Goodyear-welted shoemaker; one of Spain's best-known luxury footwear exports.
Classic engraved charm for shoe-care kits or gifting boxes.

Lottusse

Premium / established
Mallorca · heritage leather (est. 1877)
Nearly 150-year-old leather house; traditional craftsmanship, both footwear and leather goods.
Heritage-crest-style charm for anniversary or archive capsules.
lottusse.com

TLB Mallorca

Emerging designer
Mallorca (Inca) · handcrafted menswear shoes
Founded by Toni Llobera Barceló; shoes made entirely from island-based resources and workshops.
Small hallmark-style charm reflecting island provenance.
tlbmallorca.com

Pons / Avarcas

Affordable luxury
Menorca · leather sandals
Menorcan artisan-made avarca sandals; simple forms, strong summer-uniform status.
Ankle-strap sandal charm for a warm-weather capsule.

Magrit

Premium / established
Elda (Alicante) · women's luxury footwear
Established Spanish women's shoe house known for craftsmanship out of the Elda shoemaking region.
Heel or buckle-echo charm for evening lines.

Chie Mihara

Emerging designer
Elda (Alicante) · design-forward footwear
Japanese-Spanish designer known for sculptural, color-blocked, architectural shoe design.
Colour-blocked charm mirroring a signature shoe palette.

Karine Arabian

Paris — not Spain
Paris, France (est. 2000) · footwear & accessories
French-Armenian designer known for rounded-toe shoes and accessories; has designed for Madonna and jewelry for Chanel. Requested addition — flagged since it sits outside this doc's Spain-based criteria, sold through Spanish retailers like Spartoo.
Statement charm for a capsule collection, if pursuing outside Spain's borders.

Spa & Wellness

12 venues

A separate collab track from fashion — charms here work as boutique/gift-shop retail, robe or key-fob charms, and treatment-programme keepsakes rather than garment add-ons. Gold marks world-renowned flagships, teal marks smaller boutique/emerging retreats, terracotta marks heritage thermal & thalasso houses.

SHA Wellness Clinic

Flagship
Altea, Alicante · medical wellness
Cliffside clinic over the Mediterranean, widely ranked the world's leading medical wellness destination.
Longevity-programme graduation charm as a keepsake gift.

Six Senses Ibiza

Flagship
Cala Xarraca, Ibiza · resort & longevity
1,200sqm spa and RoseBar Longevity Centre blending biohacking with shamanic healing on Ibiza's quiet north coast.
Boutique retail charm sold alongside spa amenity kits.

Finca Cortesín Spa

Flagship
Casares, Málaga · destination resort spa
2,200sqm spa with thermal baths and multi-national therapist team, part of the wider Finca Cortesín estate.
Robe-tie or slipper charm for suite turndown gifting.

Lanserhof Marbella

Flagship
Finca Cortesín estate, Marbella · medical wellness (opening late 2026)
24,000sqm F.X. Mayr Cure facility opening within Finca Cortesín — positioned as Southern Europe's most advanced medical health resort.
Launch-capsule charm timed to the late-2026 opening.

Marbella Club

Flagship
Marbella, Málaga · classic beachfront resort
One of Spain's most exclusive beachfront resorts, understated luxury with a long-standing wellness offer.
Beach-bag charm for poolside/spa gift sets.

Camiral Golf & Wellness

Flagship
Costa Brava, Girona · golf & wellness resort
300-hectare countryside resort pairing traditional holistic therapies with technology-led treatments.
Members'-club charm as a loyalty or milestone gift.

Shanti-Som Wellbeing Retreat

Boutique retreat
Ojén, near Marbella · yoga & holistic retreat
Small Asian-inspired retreat in the hills above Marbella built around yoga, meditation and personalized care.
Mala-style charm tied to a signature meditation programme.

Teranka Formentera

Boutique retreat
Formentera, Balearic Islands · living-art wellness
Boutique retreat set between pine forest and protected dunes, blending art, design and intuitive wellbeing.
Artist-collab charm echoing the retreat's rotating installations.

Palasiet Thalasso Spa

Heritage thermal
Benicàssim, Costa del Azahar · thalassotherapy (50+ years)
Seawater thalassotherapy house set in a 19th-century villa, one of Spain's longest-running thalasso specialists.
Sea-glass or shell-form charm matching the seawater treatment story.

Ourense Thermal Belt

Heritage thermal
Ourense, Galicia · natural hot springs district
Europe's second-largest thermal flow after Budapest; public riverside springs (Outariz, A Chavasqueira) plus 20+ spa hotels across the province.
Regional-crest charm for a Galicia thermal-route partnership.

URSO Hotel & Spa

Flagship
Madrid · urban luxury spa hotel
Contemporary five-star urban sanctuary in central Madrid, spa built into the hotel's core narrative.
City-break charm sold at check-in or spa reception.

Gran Hotel La Florida

Flagship
Tibidabo, Barcelona · urban resort spa
Hilltop Barcelona hotel with panoramic city views, indoor/outdoor pool and an L'Occitane spa.
Skyline-motif charm as a Barcelona arrival gift.
Notes for outreach. Brands marked ★ your list are the ones you named — the rest were selected to sit alongside them on positioning, category, and Spain roots. Status, HQ, and founding details were checked via web search in July 2026, but Instagram handles, current collaboration contacts, and press/PR emails weren't independently verified here — confirm those on each brand's or venue's official site before reaching out. A few (Magrit, Chie Mihara, Meller) are included on strong general knowledge rather than a fresh source check this pass; the same caveat applies to Gran Hotel La Florida in the Spa & Wellness section — worth a quick sanity check before pitching.
